Residents Welfare Association,
MPCHS B-17

A non-government, non-profit private organization that provides a platform to dicuss problems
of the residents of MPCHS B-17 and find resolve them in an organized manner.

Aims & Objectives

  1. Residents Welfare Assocation MPCHS B-17, Islamabad is a non-government, non-profit, private organization. Its primary objective is to provide a platform to the members to discuss common problems of the residents and find out ways and means to address them in an organized manner.
  2. To achieve the above , RWA aims at cultivating a spirit of mutual help and goodwill amongst the residents of sector B-17 in general and the members of RWA in particular.
  3. The RWA B-17 focuses on lawful acts, and deeds or things , that are considered necessary to provide better civic living to the residents. Any unlawful or unethical activities are discouraged by the Association.
  4. The RWA will, by and large act as a focal point for the management of MPCHS to address communal problems of the residents of sector B-17 Islamabad. It will act as a buffer between the management of MPCHS and its members. However, purely individual issues will not fall in the domain of RWA.
  5. The RWA will identify problems faced by the members for the solutions by the MPCHS. If the management of MPCHS grossly fails to address the lawful issues, then RWA may choose to seek the help of controlling authorities of MPCHS as well of the relevant courts as deemed appropriate.
  6. These are just general points. Nonetheless, management of RWA will use its best judgment to include any additional points to its domain which are considered relevant to the welfare of its members and inform the management of MPCHS.
